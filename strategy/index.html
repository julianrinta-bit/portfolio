Professional Services · Fractional CFO
2024 · 6-month engagement
From posting into the void to $216K in retainers and a 4-month waitlist.
A fractional CFO with 15 years of experience had been trying YouTube for 8 months. 38 videos, 870 subscribers, one inbound lead who ghosted. Content was technically excellent but felt like a finance lecture—retention dropped at the 40-second mark.
Diagnosed the core problem: content answered questions nobody was searching for. She was making videos for other CFOs, not for the founders who would hire her. Rebuilt strategy around founder anxieties—cash runway fears, fundraising mistakes, "when to fire your bookkeeper." Created a "founder financial mistakes" series with real anonymized scenarios.

Real Estate · Luxury Residential · Miami
2024 · 10-month engagement
Zillow spend cut to zero. 3 closings from YouTube—$2.1M in transactions.
A Miami luxury real estate agent spending $8K/month on Zillow leads with diminishing returns. Every lead was cold. Every call started from zero trust. The agent needed a channel where buyers arrived pre-sold.
Built YouTube as the primary acquisition channel: hyperlocal neighborhood deep dives, weekly market intelligence, buyer education, and lifestyle/relocation guides. The confession format ("I told my client to wait—it cost them $40K") outperformed every other title pattern.
