Professional Services · Fractional CFO
2024 · 8 months
From posting into the void to $18K/month in new contracts and a 4-month waitlist.
A fractional CFO with 15 years of experience and strong client results had been trying YouTube for 8 months. 38 videos, 870 subscribers, one inbound lead who ghosted. Content was technically excellent but felt like a finance lecture — retention dropped at the 40-second mark.
Diagnosed the core problem: content answered questions nobody was searching for. She was making videos for other CFOs, not for the founders who would hire her. Rebuilt strategy around founder anxieties — cash runway fears, fundraising mistakes, "when to fire your bookkeeper." Shortened videos from 18 to 7-9 minutes. Created a "founder financial mistakes" series that opened with real anonymized client scenarios.

Real Estate · Luxury Residential
2024 · 10 months
Zillow spend cut to zero. 3 closings from YouTube — $2.1M in transactions.
A Miami luxury real estate agent spending $8K/month on Zillow leads with diminishing returns. Every lead was cold. Every call started from zero trust. The agent needed a channel where buyers arrived pre-sold — having already watched neighborhood tours, market analysis, and pricing breakdowns.
Built YouTube as the primary acquisition channel: hyperlocal neighborhood deep dives, weekly market intelligence updates, buyer education content, and lifestyle/relocation guides. Each video positioned the agent as the definitive market authority. The confession format ("I told my client to wait — it cost them $40K") outperformed every other title pattern.

What I Build

Systems that scale YouTube operations.

What I'd deploy at a YouTube agency running 30+ client channels.

Channel-Level P&L Dashboards

Every client channel gets a content-to-revenue scorecard — median views, leads generated, cash collected per video. Not monthly reports; live attribution so strategists make decisions from real numbers.

Scalable Ideation System

AI-powered research pipelines that surface competitor gaps and high-intent topics. At 100+ videos/month, no single person should be the bottleneck on "what video to make next." I've solved this across 50+ channels.

International Expansion

Clients sell globally but publish in English only. I've scaled content across 15 languages with 2-3x ROI on localized versions. Even 3-4 languages can double inbound pipeline without doubling cost.

Kill Discipline

75% proven formats, 25% experimental — with tracking to know within 2-3 videos whether an experiment is working, and the discipline to cut it same week if it isn't.
